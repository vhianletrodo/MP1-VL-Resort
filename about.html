Our History

From the Start

Tucked away in the heart of our charming suburban subdivision, our hotel resort first opened its doors in 2013. This small yet cozy inn was built on the vision of providing a peaceful oasis for residents and travelers alike. From the very beginning, our warm and inviting atmosphere made us a favorite choice for those seeking a tranquil escape. As word of our hospitality spread, so did our reputation, leading to our expansion in 2023.

With this expansion came additional rooms, a beautiful pool and spa area, and a restaurant that delighted guests with delectable cuisine. We quickly evolved into a cherished retreat, offering not only relaxation but also a sense of belonging to our close-knit community. Today, our resort stands as a testament to our commitment to creating memorable experiences for all who visit.

Our rich history is a part of the tapestry of the subdivision itself, and we remain dedicated to preserving the values and charm that have defined us since our inception. As we look back on our journey, we eagerly await the opportunity to be a part of your future memories, continuing to offer comfort, relaxation, and a true sense of home within our beloved neighborhood.

  • Vivian Letrodo
    CEO
