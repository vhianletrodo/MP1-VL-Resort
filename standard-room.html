Simple Comfort, Memorable Stay

Standard Room

Our Standard Rooms offer a perfect blend of comfort and practicality, making them an ideal choice for solo travelers or couples seeking a cozy and inviting space. Each room is designed to provide all the essentials for a restful stay, ensuring that your visit is both enjoyable and hassle-free.

These rooms feature a well-appointed layout, with a comfortable bed and a private bathroom to meet your basic needs. The decor is tasteful and the atmosphere welcoming, offering a pleasant retreat after a day of exploration or business meetings.

Our Standard Rooms are a testament to our commitment to ensuring that all guests experience a stay that is characterized by simplicity, convenience, and comfort. With all the essentials at your fingertips, you can focus on enjoying your time at VL Resort.

Rates from Php2,000.00 per night
50% Deposit Required
1 night minimum stay

Amenities

  • Air Conditioned Room
  • Hot/Cold Shower
  • Bath Amenities
  • Telephone
  • Television

Thoughtful Touches

  • Coffee and Tea making facilities
  • Bedroom slippers
  • Room Service
Maximum Occupancy with Charges: 2 Adults or 1 Adult and 1 Child (0-5 years of age)

Fees & Policies

Refundable Deposits
  • Deposit is payable by bank transfer and is due within 72 hours of booking the reservation
Optional Extras
  • Local Cuisine Breakfast is offered for an extra charge of approximately PHP 150 for adults and PHP 150 for children
  • Airport Shuttle Service is offered for an extra charge of PHP 900 per person (one-way)
  • Early check-in can be arranged for an extra charge (subject to availability)
  • Housekeeping is offered for an extra charge
Pets
  • Pets are allowed for an extra charge of PHP 500 per accommodation (may vary based on length of stay)
Hygiene & Cleanliness
  • This property advises that enhanced cleaning and guest safety measures are currently in place.
  • Disinfectant is used to clean the property.
  • Social distancing measures are in place; staff at the property wear personal protective equipment; guests are provided with hand sanitizer.
  • Contactless check-out is available.
  • Individually-wrapped food options are available for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, and also through room service.
  • Each guestroom is kept vacant for a minimum of 72 hours between bookings.
Policies
  • Noise-free guestrooms cannot be guaranteed.
  • Only registered guests are allowed in the guestrooms.
  • Guests can rest easy knowing there's a fire extinguisher, a smoke detector, a security system, and a first aid kit on site.
  • The name on the credit card used at check-in to pay for incidentals must be the primary name on the guestroom reservation.
  • This property accepts credit cards, debit cards and cash.

Why Stay at Our Resort with Our Standard Rooms

At our resort, we understand that sometimes, less is more. Our Standard Rooms are designed for travelers who appreciate simplicity and comfort. These rooms provide all the essentials for a peaceful and enjoyable stay, ensuring that your visit is both relaxing and hassle-free.

If you're looking for a cost-effective choice without sacrificing quality, our Standard Rooms offer excellent value for your money. You can rest easy knowing that you'll enjoy a clean, comfortable, and well-designed space without breaking the bank.

Our Standard Rooms are cozy and inviting, making them perfect for solo travelers or couples seeking a convenient and budget-friendly accommodation option. Whether you're here for business or leisure, these rooms provide a peaceful retreat.
